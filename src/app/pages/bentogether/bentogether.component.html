Bentogether

Food education system

Overview

BenTogether is a digital bento box paired with a mobile application, developed as part of HCDE's senior capstone project. Its purpose is to teach families about food education, while strengthening family relationships through the lunch making process.

Role

In a team of five, I worked as the software developer for the mobile application. In addition, I was a user researcher conducting surveys, qualitative analsysis, and user testing.
